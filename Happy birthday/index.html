Red velvet Bday cake recipe

Cake ingredients

  • 1 1/3 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp red food coloring
  • 1 cup buttermilk

Cake steps

  • Preheat oven 175°C
  • In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt
  • In a large bowl, mix together the sugar and butter until it's light and fluffy. Mix in the eggs, vanilla, and red food coloring
  • Combine the medium bowl with the large bowl and add the buttermilk
  • Bake for 25-30 min, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean
  • Let the cake cool before adding the cream

Cream Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 3 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/2 vanilla extract
  • 3-4 tbsp milk

Cream steps

  • Mix together butter, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until it's light and fluffy
  • Add milk, one tablespoon at a time, until frosting reaches desired consistency
  • Add the cream to the cake
  • Add candles to the cake and light them up!
